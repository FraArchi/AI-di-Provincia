# Features Research

## Table Stakes (Must Have)
- **Article Public Listing & Reading**: View articles on Home and Article pages.
- **Markdown Rendering**: Parse `##` and basic formatting.
- **SEO Meta Tags**: Open Graph, Title, Description per article.
- **Responsive UI**: Mobile-first design for reading.

## Differentiators
- **Private/Gated Area (`/riservato/`)**: Content accessible only via newsletter subscription or direct login.
- **Value Ladder Monetization**: Ebook/course offerings hidden behind the gated area.
- **Multilingual Support**: Subdomains (`it.`, `es.`, etc.) for scalable traffic.

## Anti-Features
- **Complex CMS Backend**: Avoid building a custom CMS; use Supabase dashboard or a simple admin route.
- **Ads on Public Face**: No AdSense early on to avoid ruining UX and SEO trust.
