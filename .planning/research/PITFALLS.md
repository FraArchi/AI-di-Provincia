# Pitfalls Research

## Common Mistakes
- **Relying on SPA for SEO**: Google struggles with pure client-side React apps. *Prevention*: Implement SSG (e.g., Vike, Next.js) or pre-rendering.
- **Over-monetizing too early**: Adding ads before 30k visits/month ruins trust. *Prevention*: Wait and use premium networks (Ezoic/Mediavine) later.
- **100% AI Content**: Google penalizes pure AI content. *Prevention*: Always use the 70/30 rule with 25-30% human editing (the "Faraone" method).
