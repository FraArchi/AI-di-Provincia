# Research Summary

## Key Findings
**Stack:** React/Vite/Supabase (existing), needs SSG/SSR capability for SEO. ConvertKit for newsletter.
**Table Stakes:** Public article reading, markdown rendering, responsive UI, SEO meta tags.
**Watch Out For:** Pure SPA SEO issues, over-monetization too early, 100% AI content penalties.

## Strategic Direction
Evolve the current "AI di Provincia" SPA into the "Faraone con sterzo da strada" hybrid model: a pristine, SEO-friendly public face combined with a highly monetized, gated private area accessible via newsletter subscription.
